ISHA ADAA

    1) Ada karen: 4 rakaat, Isha, Sunnat

    2) Ada karen: 4 rakaat, Isha, Farz

    3) Ada karen: 2 rakaat, Isha, Sunnat

    4) Ada karen: 2 rakaat, Isha, Nafl

 

TARAWEEH NIYAT + IBTEDA (Begin)

Ideally: offer behind Hafiz-e-Quraan


else: behind any ba-sharaa (one who in-deed follows shariat) person or: offer alone.


Suggestion if not Hafiz: Recite in order from Alam tara kaifa to Surah An-nas in rakaats 1 to 10 and then repeat the same in the next 10 rakaats (Surahs at the end of this document)


*PLEASE NOTE: THESE DUAs and SURAHs HAVE BEEN PRESENTED HERE IN ROMAN ENGLISH FOR EASE, BUT PLEASE MAKE SURE TO EITHER HEAR A RECITATION OF THE SAME OR TRY TO READ AND LEARN THE ARABIC VERSIONS OF THESE WITH HELP OF SOMEONE KNOWLEDGEABLE

    5) Niyat karen: 20 rakaat, Taraweeh Sunnat, 2 at a time

Niyat ki maine, Namaaze taraweeh Sunnat ki, waaste Allah taalaa ke, muh mera Kaabatullah sharrief ke taraf, Allah hu akbar

    6) Ibteda karen:

Assalatu sunnat-ut-taraveeh yarahamakumulla wa laa ilaa ha il lallalhu wallahu akbar allahu akbar walilla hil-hamd

    8) Dua padhen (1 martaba(times)):

         Ashadu allailaha illallahu wahdahu laashareekalahu wa ashhaduanna Mohammadan abduhu wa rasooluhu. Aameen

Surah Al-Fil to Surah An-nas


Surah Al-Fil

  1. Alam tara kaifa fa'ala rabbuka bi ashaabil feel
  2. Alam yaj'al kaidahum fee tadleel
  3. Wa arsala 'alaihim tairan abaabeel
  4. Tarmeehim bihijaaratim min sijjeel
  5. Faja 'alahum ka'asfim m'akool

 

Surah Quraish

  1. Li-eelaafi quraish
  2. Eelaafihim rihlatash shitaaa'i wassaif
  3. Faly'abudoo rabba haazal-bait
  4. Allazeee at'amahum min joo'inw-wa-aamanahum min khawf

Surah Al-Maun

  1. Ara 'aytal lazee yukazzibu biddeen
  2. Fazaalikal lazee yadu'ul-yateem
  3. Wa la yahuddu 'alaa ta'aamil miskeen
  4. Fa wailul-lil musalleen
  5. Allazeena hum 'an salaatihim saahoon
  6. Allazeena hum yuraaa'oon
  7. Wa yamna'oonal maa'oon

Surah Al-Kauthar

  1. Innaaa a'tainaa kal kauthar
  2. Fasalli li rabbika wanhar
  3. Inna shaani'aka huwal abtar

Surah Al-Kafirun

  1. Qul yaaa-ayyuhal kaafiroon
  2. Laaa a'budu maa t'abudoon
  3. Wa laaa antum 'aabidoona maaa a'bud
  4. Wa laaa ana 'abidum maa 'abattum
  5. Wa laaa antum 'aabidoona maaa a'bud
  6. Lakum deenukum wa liya deen.

Surah An-Nasr

  1. Iza jaaa'a nasrul-laahi walfath
  2. Wa ra-aitan naasa yadkhuloona fee deenil laahi afwajaa
  3. Fasabbih bihamdi rabbika wastaghfirh, innahoo kaana tawwaaba

Surah Al-Lahab

  1. Tabbat yadaa abee Lahabinw-wa tabb
  2. Maa aghnaa 'anhu maaluhoo wa ma kasab
  3. Sa-yaslaa naaran zaata lahab
  4. Wamra-atuhoo hammaa latal-hatab
  5. Fee jeedihaa hablum mim-masad

Surah Al-Ikhlas

  1. Qul huwal laahu ahad
  2. Allah hus-samad
  3. Lam yalid wa lam yoolad
  4. Wa lam yakul-lahoo kufuwan ahad

Surah Al-Falaq

  1. Qul a'oozu bi rabbil-falaq
  2. Min sharri maa khalaq
  3. Wa min sharri ghasiqin iza waqab
  4. Wa min sharrin-naffaa-thaati fil 'uqad
  5. Wa min sharri haasidin izaa hasad

Surah An-Nas

  1. Qul a'oozu birabbin naas
  2. Malikin naas
  3. Ilaahin naas
  4. Min sharril waswaasil khannaas
  5. Allazee yuwaswisu fee sudoorin naas
  6. Minal jinnati wannaas
